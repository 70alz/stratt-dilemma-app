📝 The PREEP Method

Use this framework to build a strong argument. Fill in each section:

P Point: What do you think?

Starter: "I think..."

R Reason: Why do you think this?

Starter: "This is because..."

E Evidence: What part of the story helps prove your point?

Starter: "In the story..."

E Explanation: How does your evidence prove your reason?

Starter: "This shows that..."

P Point: Wrap it up!

Starter: "So, I believe..."
